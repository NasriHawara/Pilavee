About Pilavee

Our passion is to guide you towards strength, balance, and well-being.

Our Story & Philosophy

I’ve always dreamed of a workout space that felt like me calming, feminine, and thoughtfully designed. A place where movement meets peace, and where every detail from the soft lighting to the overall energy was created to help you feel safe, supported, and at ease. That dream became Pilavee. I wanted more than just a studio; I wanted a space where women could reconnect with their bodies in a graceful, empowering way. To bring that vision to life, I made sure to handpick a team of the most talented and caring instructors women who guide with heart and experience. Every class at Pilavee is a reflection of that intention: mindful, elegant, and made with love.
